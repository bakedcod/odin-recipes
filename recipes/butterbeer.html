Butterbeer

Description:

Everyone has heard of butterbeer...right?

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (8 oz) club soda or cream soda
  • ½ cup (4 oz) butterscotch syrup (ice cream topping)
  • ½ tablespoon butter (softened)
  • Heavy cream (optional)

Steps:

  1. Combine softened butter and butterscotch syrup in bowl
  2. Add club/cream soda to bowl
  3. In a seperate bowl whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form
  4. Pour Butterbeer mixture into a mug and top with Whipped Cream
